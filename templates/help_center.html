Help Center

Browse products, click "Add to Cart", go to your cart, and click "Buy Now" to proceed with address and payment.

Currently, orders cannot be canceled once placed. Please contact support for assistance.

We support Cash on Delivery, Google Pay, and Paytm for secure and easy checkout.

Delivery usually takes 3–7 business days depending on your location and seller processing time.

You earn coins on every order. Once you collect 100 coins, you’re eligible for a 10% discount at checkout.

Go to your Profile page, edit your details in the form, and click “Save Changes.”

Once you're registered as a seller, go to your Seller Dashboard and click "Add Product" to list new items.

Go to the Delivery Dashboard and accept available orders. Once accepted, update delivery status upon completion.

Click “Change Password” on the Profile page. You'll receive a password reset email with instructions.
